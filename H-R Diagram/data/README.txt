"planets_2019.03.22_15.23.49.csv" is data downloaded from NASA's exoplanet research site under "confirmed planets" at https://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/
"wikipedia-data.csv" is data taken from wikipedia at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_exoplanetary_host_stars
"nasa-data.csv" and "wiki-data.csv" are trimmed data with calculated absolute-magnitude from original data
"combined-data" combines "nasa-data.csv" and "wiki-data.csv"
